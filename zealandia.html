Zealandia plans to work restoring the valley it is in for the next 500 years.
It has helped many different animals return to the Wellington area.

Click on the animals below for a fun fact!

kākāriki

Kākāriki make loud chattering sounds to each other while they fly.

Tuatara

Tuatara have a third eye on their forehead that they use to sense light.

Kākā

Kākā have a very strong beak, which they can use to tear through wood.

Wētā

Giant wētā are the world's heaviest insect, and can weigh more than a mouse.

Takahē

Takahē were once thought to be extinct, until they were found again in 1948. However, there are still only about 350 left.
